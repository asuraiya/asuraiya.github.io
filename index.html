The Four Pillars of Successful Philanthropy.

Incentive

You need an incentive to want to donate to a cause. How's "It makes you feel like a superhero" for a reason? Impactful helps you discover your hidden philanthropic superpowers.

Discovery

What causes and organizations deserve your super-attention? We bring you the charities making the most impact in improving the lives of the less fortunate around the world.

Donation

Impactful takes the pain out of the act of donating your hard-earned money. We support your favorite mobile payment processors, and get the money to your organization of choice, pronto.

Feedback

Ever wonder what happened to the money you donated? Wonder no more. Get monthly report cards delivered straight to your mobile device. Empowering, informative, and unobtrusive.
